# Figma Plugins for iOS Design

Plugins that actually help when designing iOS apps. Skipping the generic ones.

---

## SF Symbols

- [SF Symbols Browser](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1413569685183139719) — Browse and insert any SF Symbol directly onto your canvas. The fastest way to work with SF Symbols in Figma.
- [SF Symbols PRO](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1579706526435398788/sf-symbols-pro-14-000-font-icons) — 14,000+ SF Symbols rendered as SVG. Works without needing the SF Symbols font installed — useful for Windows-side collaboration too.
- [SF Symbol Generator](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1487107089082698461/sf-symbol-generator) — Go the other direction: convert your custom Figma icon into an SF Symbol SVG ready to import into the SF Symbols app or Xcode.
- [SF Symbols Optimizer](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1265484574589944515/sf-symbols-optimizer) — Exports SF Symbols from Figma as properly formatted SVGs compatible with SF Symbols 5, 6, and 7.

---

## Layout & iOS Structure

- [iOS Safe Area Guide](https://www.figma.com/community/file/1425591247196745890/ios-safe-area-guide-iphone-17-and-older) — Community file (not a plugin) with safe area frames for every iPhone including iPhone 17. Drop it into your project as a reference layer.
- [Design Scale](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/845852059073543928/design-scale) — Quickly scale frames and groups proportionally — useful when adapting iPhone designs to iPad sizes.

---

## Accessibility & Contrast

- [Stark](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/732603254453395948/stark-contrast-accessibility-checker) — The go-to accessibility plugin. Checks contrast ratios, simulates color blindness, and audits touch target sizes. Essential before any handoff.
- [A11y - Focus Order](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/731310036968334777/a11y-focus-order) — Annotates the reading/focus order of your design — important for VoiceOver support.

---

## Content & Realistic Data

- [Content Reel](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/731627216655469406/content-reel) — Fill text layers with realistic names, addresses, phone numbers, and avatars. Makes designs look finished instead of "Lorem ipsum".
- [Unsplash](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/738454987945972471/unsplash) — Insert free, high-quality photos directly into Figma. Faster than leaving the app.

---

## Dark Mode

- [Dark Mode Magic](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/834062945643780423/dark-mode-magic) — Generates a dark mode version of your design by automatically mapping light colors to dark equivalents. Good starting point before fine-tuning manually.

---

## Design Quality

- [Design Lint](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/801195587640428208/design-lint) — Finds inconsistencies in your file: missing fills, mismatched corner radii, text not using styles, etc. Run it before every developer handoff.
- [Similayer](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/735733267883397781/similayer) — Select all layers with similar properties (same fill, same font size, same stroke). Massive time saver for bulk edits.
- [Clean Document](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/767379019764649932/clean-document) — Removes empty groups, flattens unnecessary structure, and resets overrides. Keeps your file fast and presentable.

---

## Typography

- [Typescale](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/739825414752646970/typescales) — Set up a type scale based on a ratio. Useful for establishing an iOS Dynamic Type-compatible type system.
- [Font Scale](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/741831543132906424/font-scale) — Preview and apply iOS Dynamic Type text sizes across your design using Apple's built-in text style system.

---

## Icons (beyond SF Symbols)

- [Iconify](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/735098390272716381/iconify) — Access 200,000+ icons from Phosphor, Material, Feather, Heroicons, and more — all without leaving Figma. Useful for mockups where SF Symbols don't apply.

---

## Handoff & Export

- [Zeplin](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1206888948756280890/zeplin) — Push frames from Figma directly to Zeplin for developer handoff, with iOS-specific measurements and UIColor values auto-generated.
- [Slicer 4](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1073307499084545649/slicer) — Batch-export slices at multiple resolutions (@1x, @2x, @3x) in one click. Much faster than manual export for asset prep.

---

## Mockups

- [Rotato](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1009741687635558607/rotato-macos-3d-mockups-explosions) — Generate 3D iPhone and Mac device mockups directly from Figma frames. Sends them to the Rotato desktop app for final rendering.
- [Mockup](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/817043359134136295/mockup) — Wrap your screen design into a device frame without leaving Figma. Supports iPhone 16, iPad, and more.
