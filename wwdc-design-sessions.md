# WWDC Design Sessions

The best sessions from Apple's Worldwide Developers Conference for designers building iOS apps. Organized by topic so you can jump to what matters most right now.

All videos are free at [developer.apple.com/videos](https://developer.apple.com/videos/design/).

---

## Liquid Glass & The New Design System

Required watching if you're designing for iOS 26.

- [Meet Liquid Glass](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2025/219/) — WWDC 2025. The introduction of Apple's biggest design language shift since iOS 7 went flat. Explains what Liquid Glass is, why it exists, and how to think about it in your own apps.
- [Get to know the new design system](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2025/356/) — WWDC 2025. The companion to "Meet Liquid Glass" — goes deeper on visual design changes, information architecture updates, and how core system components have changed.
- [Design foundations from idea to interface](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2025/359/) — WWDC 2025. Apple designers walk through the design process: how to go from a concept to a polished iOS interface. Foundational for any level.
- [Principles of great design](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2026/250/) — WWDC 2026. Apple's latest thinking on what makes great design, updated for the Liquid Glass era.

## App Icons

- [Create icons with Icon Composer](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2025/361/) — WWDC 2025. The definitive guide to using Apple's Icon Composer tool — how to export from your design tool, apply Liquid Glass effects, and preview across dark, tinted, and light appearances.
- [App Icon Design](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2017/822/) — WWDC 2017. Still one of the clearest sessions on what makes a great iOS app icon. Covers shape, color, contrast, and how icons work within the home screen context.

## SF Symbols

SF Symbols gets a major update almost every year. Watch the latest, then go back to the original.

- [What's new in SF Symbols 7](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2025/337/) — WWDC 2025. 6,900+ symbols, new animation styles, and gradient rendering. Covers the updates shipped alongside iOS 26.
- [What's new in SF Symbols 6](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2024/10188/) — WWDC 2024. New animation presets, wiggle, rotate, breathe — and how to apply them.
- [What's new in SF Symbols 5](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2023/10197/) — WWDC 2023. Symbol animations as a design tool — making interfaces feel alive.
- [What's new in SF Symbols 4](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2022/10157/) — WWDC 2022. Variable color and automatic rendering modes.
- [Adopt Variable Color in SF Symbols](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2022/10158/) — WWDC 2022. Deep dive into variable color — how to use it and how to build it into custom symbols.
- [Introducing SF Symbols](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2019/206/) — WWDC 2019. Where it all started. Still worth watching to understand the design intent behind the whole system.

## Typography

- [The details of UI typography](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2020/10175/) — WWDC 2020. One of the best sessions Apple has ever done. Covers Dynamic Type, optical size, tracking, line height, and all the small decisions that separate readable from great. Watch this one carefully.

---

## Motion & Interactions

- [Designing Fluid Interfaces](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2018/803/) — WWDC 2018. Legendary session. Apple engineers explain how gesture-driven interfaces work and why physics-based animation is core to iOS feel. Completely changed how many designers think about interaction. A must-watch even years later.

---

## Design Process & Working with SwiftUI

- [Design with SwiftUI](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2023/10115/) — WWDC 2023. Apple designers show how working directly in SwiftUI influenced the design of Maps in watchOS 10. Great insight into how design and code blur together at Apple.
- [What's new in iOS Design](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2019/808/) — WWDC 2019. Introduces the design thinking behind modal cards, contextual menus, and the big visual changes in iOS 13. Explains the "why" behind patterns that are still standard today.

---

## Timeless Principles

These sessions are older but the thinking holds up completely.

- [Essential Design Principles](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2017/802/) — WWDC 2017. Apple designers walk through the principles that underpin great software design: clarity, deference, depth, and how they translate to real decisions. Excellent for designers at any stage.
- [Designing Across Platforms](https://developer.apple.com/videos/play/wwdc2017/804/) — WWDC 2017. How to adapt a design across iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, and Apple TV without just scaling it up or down. Covers layout, input, and experience differences.
